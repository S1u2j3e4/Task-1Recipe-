Mango Cup Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 Cup unsalted butter,softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1Cup Mango puree
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F(180°)
  2. Line a 1/2 cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  3. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  4. Add a softened butter, eggs, mango puree, and Vanilla extract.
  5. Divide batter evenly amoung muffin cups.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpic comes out clean.
